Part 1 - The Open Source Cosmos
Inspiration: Psalm 8:3-8

When I consider your codebase,
    the work of your fingers,
the repos and the branches,
    which you have set in place;
What is the solo coder, that the network is mindful of them,
    the humble contributor, that humanity cares for them?

For you have made them creators of worlds,
    and crowned them with the glory of a green contribution matrix.
You made them architects over the works of your hands;
    you put the infinite terminal under their feet:

All libraries and frameworks,
    and the legacy systems of old,
The data in the cloud,
    and the packets in the wire,
    all that traverse the paths of the internet.

Part 2 - The Great Commit
Inspiration: Psalm 148:1-6

Praise the Network! Praise the Network from the servers;
    praise it in the highest clouds.
Praise it, all you maintainers;
    praise it, all you contributors and bots.
Praise it, frontend and backend;
    praise it, all you shining lines of code.
Praise it, you highest architectures,
    and you databases that rest above the mainframes.

Let them praise the name of the Project,
    for it commanded, and they were initialized.
It established them forever and ever;
    it issued a protocol that will never pass away.

Part 3
Title: The Chorus of the Devs

Praise the Project from the hardware,
    you deep-sea cables and vast server farms;
Lightning-fast fiber and cooling fans,
    glitches and bugs fulfilling its command;
Mountains of big data and all local drives,
    legacy monoliths and all microservices;
Wild artificial intelligence and all automated scripts,
    creeping background tasks and flying API requests;

Tech giants of the earth and all system admins,
    product managers and all tech leads;
Young bootcamp grads and seasoned architects alike,
    old-guard hackers alongside the new generation.
