When I consider your codebase,
    the work of your fingers,
the repos and the branches,
    which you have set in place;
What is the solo coder, that the network is mindful of them,
    the humble contributor, that humanity cares for them?

For you have made them creators of worlds,
    and crowned them with the glory of a green contribution matrix.
You made them architects over the works of your hands;
    you put the infinite terminal under their feet:

All libraries and frameworks,
    and the legacy systems of old,
The data in the cloud,
    and the packets in the wire,
    all that traverse the paths of the internet.
